Our Clearing Process

From Japan to Kampala - we handle every step with precision and expertise

1

Sourcing from Japan

We source high-quality vehicles directly from Japanese auctions and dealerships. Our team inspects each vehicle to ensure it meets our quality standards before purchase.

2

Shipping to Mombasa

Your vehicle is securely loaded onto container ships for transport to Mombasa Port. We handle all export documentation and provide tracking information throughout the journey.

3

Mombasa Port Clearance

Upon arrival, our clearing agents in Mombasa handle port clearance procedures, payment of duties and taxes, and ensure compliance with all Kenyan customs regulations.

4

Transport to Kampala

We arrange secure road transport from Mombasa to Kampala using specialized car carriers. Your vehicle is tracked throughout the journey for peace of mind.

5

Bond Storage & Final Clearance

Vehicles are stored in our bonded warehouse in Kampala while we complete final customs clearance. We handle all Ugandan customs procedures and documentation.

6

Delivery or Sale

Once cleared, you can pick up your vehicle or we can deliver it to you. Alternatively, we can sell the vehicle on your behalf through our premium sales channel.
