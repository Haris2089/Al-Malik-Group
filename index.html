Seamless Vehicle Clearing from Japan to Uganda

At Al-Malik Group, we handle the entire import and clearance process with expertise. From Mombasa Port to our bonded warehouse in Kampala, we ensure your vehicle clears customs efficiently.

We specialize in clearing vehicles from Japan and can even sell them on your behalf through our premium sales channel.

Our Clearing Process

Japan to Mombasa

We source quality vehicles from Japanese auctions and arrange secure shipping to Mombasa.

Mombasa Clearance

Our agents handle Kenyan customs clearance, duties, and documentation.

Transport to Kampala

Vehicles are securely transported to our bonded warehouse in Kampala.

Bond Storage & Final Clearance

We complete Ugandan customs clearance while vehicles are safely stored.
