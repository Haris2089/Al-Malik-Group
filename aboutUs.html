Our Japan Head Office

Headquartered in Tokyo, our head office ensures quality at the source

Malik Liaqat & Pak Japan

At the heart of our sourcing operations is our Japan office led by Malik Liaqat. With over 15 years of experience in the Japanese automotive industry, Malik Liaqat ensures that every vehicle we import meets the highest standards of quality and reliability.

Our Japan Advantage

Pak Japan serves as our strategic hub for sourcing premium vehicles directly from Japanese auctions and dealerships. This direct connection allows us to:

  • Access Japan's extensive vehicle auctions and dealership networks
  • Conduct thorough 150-point inspections on every vehicle
  • Verify vehicle history and maintenance records
  • Ensure authentic Japanese specifications and features
  • Negotiate the best prices for our East African customers

Streamlined Import Process

Our Japan operations handle the entire import process from selection to shipping:

  • Vehicle sourcing and inspection
  • Documentation and customs preparation
  • Shipping coordination to Mombasa or Dar es Salaam
  • Real-time tracking of vehicle shipments
  • Pre-arrival preparation for customs clearance
